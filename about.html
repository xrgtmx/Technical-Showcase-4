What is Technical Showcase 4?

Technical Showcase 4 is a contest for StepMania / In The Groove that specializes in technical pad simfiles, such as those found in the following packs/series:

• BemaniBeats
• DVogan's Tech Support
• Mute Sims
• 3/5guys1pack
• ITGAlex Comp
• Swagesteps
• Sudziosis
• Skittles Selection
• N.E.O. Sims
• Feraligatr Scales
• Notice Me Benpai
• Valex's Magical 4-Arrow Adventure
